# Off-Page SEO: Brand Mentions

Search engines (especially AI-driven systems like Perplexity and ChatGPT) synthesize brand sentiment and authority from mentions across the web, *even if they are not hyperlinked*.

## 1. Unlinked Brand Mentions
- When a reputable media outlet or industry blog mentions the brand name but fails to hyperlink it to the homepage.
- **Action**: Run a query (`"brand name" -site:brand.com`). Find these unlinked mentions.
- **Outreach**: Politely email the editor thanking them for the feature and requesting a hyperlink to add value for their readers.

## 2. Sentiment and E-E-A-T Signals
- AI engines do not rely purely on links; they perform NLP sentiment analysis on brand contexts.
- Mentions on Trustpilot, Reddit, Quora, and G2 contribute massively to the "Trust" pillar of E-E-A-T.
- **Action**: Monitor industry forums. If users are comparing tools, naturally entering the conversation and providing value acts as an Off-Page signal.

## 3. Entity Establishment
- Ensure the CEO and key company figures are consistently mentioned together in press releases to establish entity relationships in Google's Knowledge Graph.
- This creates strong "Off-Page" validation for the author bios implemented "On-Page".
