# Off-Page SEO: Link Building

Inbound links act as "votes of confidence" across the web. They are a core ranking signal, establishing Domain Rating (DR) and Topical Trust.

## 1. Principles of a Healthy Link Profile
- **Relevance**: A backlink from a pet blog to a crypto exchange is spam. Links must come from topically cohesive sites.
- **Authority**: A link from `.edu`, `.gov`, or an industry leader (High DR) carries significantly more weight than 100 links from low DR sites.
- **Velocity**: Acquiring 500 links in 2 days from zero history looks unnatural and risks algorithmic penalties. Build progressively.

## 2. Safe Link Building Strategies
- **Digital PR**: Pitching data-driven studies or industry insights to journalists (HARO, Connectively) to secure high-tier press citations.
- **Guest Posting**: Writing high-quality authoritative content for related industry blogs. (Ensure no exchange of money or spam networks are involved).
- **Resource Page Outreach**: Finding "Best Resources for [Topic]" pages and suggesting your highly valuable guide as an addition.
- **Broken Link Building**: Finding broken outbound links on competitor/industry sites and offering your live page as a replacement.

## 3. Toxic Links & Disavowal
- If a site is hit with a negative SEO attack (thousands of spammy `.xyz` keyword-stuffed anchor links), use Ahrefs or Semrush to identify them.
- **Last Resort**: Compile a `disavow.txt` file and submit it to Google Search Console to nullify their impact. Use sparingly.
