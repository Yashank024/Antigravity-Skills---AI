# AI SEO: Generative Engine Optimization (GEO)

GEO is the practice of optimizing content specifically to be cited by RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) systems like ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews.

## 1. Fact Density
- AI models heavily favor content with high fact density. 
- **Rule**: Replace filler adjectives with hard statistics, dates, and names. 
- **Bad**: "Many users rely on our platform daily."
- **Good**: "Over 400,000 monthly active users rely on the platform as of Q3 2025."

## 2. Direct Answer Formatting
- RAG systems extract concise snippets to feed back to the LLM.
- **Agent Execution**: Always add a 40–60 word direct, bolded answer immediately following an `H2` heading before diving into deeper explanations.

## 3. Expert Quotations
- AI Overviews synthesize trust by quoting recognized industry experts.
- Embed explicit pull-quotes (`<blockquote>`) from verifiable experts within the text. Ensure the expert is named and credentialed.

## 4. Unambiguous Structure
- Do not use clever, ambiguous headings. Use highly descriptive, literal language in `H2` and `H3` tags so the AI indexer instantly understands the section's purpose without requiring complex NLP inference.
