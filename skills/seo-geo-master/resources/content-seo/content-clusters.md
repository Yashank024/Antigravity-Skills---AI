# Content SEO: Content Clusters

Content Clustering is the architectural execution of Topical Authority. It groups related content together using a rigid internal linking structure.

## 1. The Hub (Pillar Page)
- A broad, high-level guide covering the core topic (e.g., *The Complete Guide to Personal Finance*).
- **Format**: Long-form, highly structured, acts as an index.

## 2. The Spokes (Cluster Content)
- In-depth, specific articles covering sub-topics (e.g., *How to balance a checkbook*, *Best high-yield savings accounts 2026*).
- **Execution**: Every Spoke must hyperlink back to the Hub using exact or partial-match anchor text. The Hub must link down to every Spoke.

## 3. Benefits
- **Crawl Efficiency**: Spiders can rapidly parse the semantic relationship.
- **Link Equity Flow**: If one Spoke earns a powerful backlink, that equity flows up to the Hub and distributes to all other Spokes.

## Agent Audit Action
- Look for orphan blog posts and suggest merging them into a newly created Hub page to form a unified Cluster.
