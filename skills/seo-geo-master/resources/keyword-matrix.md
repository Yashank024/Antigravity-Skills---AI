# Keyword Research & Topic Authority Matrix

Use this matrix to map out the Hub-and-Spoke structure for topical authority.

## Intent Classification Reference
- **Informational**: "how does SEO work" (Blog posts, guides)
- **Navigational**: "Semrush login" (Brand/landing pages)
- **Commercial**: "best SEO tools 2025" (Comparisons, reviews)
- **Transactional**: "buy Semrush Pro" (Product pages, pricing)
- **Conversational/AI**: "what is the best way to do SEO in 2025" (FAQ-style, direct answers)

## Execution Steps
1. Identify 5-10 core seed keywords.
2. Run competitor gap analysis.
3. Group keywords into Hubs (Pillar Pages ~3000 words) and Spokes (Cluster Pages ~1000 words).
4. Ensure every Spoke links back to its Hub, and to 2-3 related Spokes.
