# On-Page SEO: Internal Linking

Internal links distribute PageRank (Authority) to interior pages and establish Topical Clusters. An orphaned page (0 internal links) carries no value.

## 1. The Hub and Spoke Architecture
- **Hub Page**: The master guide on a broad topic (e.g., *Ultimate Guide to Content Marketing*).
- **Spoke Pages**: Highly specific sub-topics (*How to write a blog post*, *Best content marketing tools*).
- **Rule**: All Spokes must link up to the Hub. The Hub must link down to all Spokes. Spokes should link laterally to each other where hyper-relevant.

## 2. Anchor Text Strategy
- Use **Descriptive Anchor Text**.
- **Bad**: Click [here] to learn about SEO.
- **Good**: Read our complete guide to [Technical SEO fundamentals].
- **Rule**: Do not use the exact same anchor text on every single link to the same page (appears unnatural/spam). Mix in variations.

## 3. Link Equity Distribution
- Link from high-traffic, high-authority pages (like the Homepage or a top-performing blog post) to new or conversion-critical pages to share authority.

## Agent Checklist
- Check for Orphaned Pages (pages with no incoming internal links) and connect them.
- Ensure main navigation links logically to structural category pages.
- Add at least 3-5 contextual internal links in every new blog post.
