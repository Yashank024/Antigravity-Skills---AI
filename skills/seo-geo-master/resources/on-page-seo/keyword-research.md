# On-Page SEO: Keyword Research

Keyword Research is the foundation of mapping user demand to website supply. We do not just target high volume; we target relevancy and conversion likelihood.

## The Semantic Core (Seed Keywords)
- Identify 3–5 core business pillars (Seeds).
- E.g., for a SaaS project management tool: `project management`, `task tracking`, `team collaboration`.

## Long-Tail Analysis (Hub & Spoke)
Instead of fighting giants for the Seed keywords, build out Long-Tail spokes:
- High intent, lower search volume variations.
- E.g., `best free project management tool for remote engineering teams`.

## Execution Steps for the Agent
1. **Analyze Current Ranks**: Look at what the domain already ranks for. Find keywords on Page 2 (Positions 11-20) and prioritize them for optimizations ("Striking Distance" keywords).
2. **Competitor Gap**: Find keywords where 3 top competitors rank, but the target domain does not.
3. **Keyword Mapping**: Ensure 1 Target Primary Keyword per URL. Avoid Keyword Cannibalization (multiple pages targeting the exact same intent).
