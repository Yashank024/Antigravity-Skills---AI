# Local SEO: Google Business Profile (GBP)

For businesses serving a specific geographic radius, the Google Business Profile (formerly GMB) is the absolute highest priority. It dictates visibility in the "Local Pack" (Map Pack).

## 1. Core Optimization Requirements
- **Primary Category**: Must be brutally accurate. Do not choose "Store" if you are a "Shoe Store".
- **NAP Consistency**: Name, Address, and Phone Number must match the website footer perfectly.
- **Description**: Fully utilize the 750-character limit. Front-load primary local keywords, but write for humans.

## 2. Active Management Signals
Google ranks active profiles higher than dormant ones.
- **Reviews**: The velocity and recency of 5-star reviews strongly influence map rankings. *Action*: Business must actively reply to all reviews (positive and negative).
- **Google Posts**: Post updates, offers, and events weekly to signal active management.
- **Q&A**: Pre-populate the Q&A section with common customer questions and answer them from the owner account.

## 3. Image Metadata (Advanced)
- Upload high-quality, unedited photos of the interior, exterior, and staff.
- Search engines can interpret EXIF data (GPS coordinates) if uploaded natively.
